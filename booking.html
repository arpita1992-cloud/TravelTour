Booking & Confirmation

Prior confirmation of booking, it is always necessary to avail our tours and services. A tour or service may be considered as confirmed only after an advance payment of 50% is made. The remaining payment has to be made as per the following deposit and payment policies. Deposit & Payment All deposits and payments will be subject to our cancellation policy. Confirmation of group (minimum 6 PAX) or exclusive bookings must be done at least 30 days before the departure. In case the tour or the service falls in the middle of long holiday (3 days or more at a stretch) this confirmation will need to be done at least 40 days prior to departure. In both cases full payment has to be made 19 days prior to departure. For individual booking 50% advance payment has to be made 15 days prior to departure. Rest of the payment has to be made 3 days before departure. For long holidays advance payment of 50% has to be made at least 19 days before departure. Rest of the payment has to be made 4 days before departure. For Hillside Resort booking one night’s payment has to be made at least 5 days before check in. Rest of the payment has to be made 3 days before check in. In case of long holidays one night’s payment has to be made at least 7 days prior to check in. Rest of the payment has to be made 4 days before check in. For vehicle hire, advance payment of 50% has to be made 5 days prior to departure. Rest of the payment has to be made 48 hours before departure. During long holiday period, 50% advance payment has to be made 7 days prior to departure a
