All our vehicles are air-conditioned.

We provide the vehicle with good and experienced driver, fuel, parking fees and small ferry charges (maximum Tk. 200.00). In case of bigger ferry charges clients must pay that on the spot or request us to pay and add that to the bill. When you will hire our transport for within the Dhaka city for the whole day, you get it for maximum of 10 hours’ time and 200 km run. When you will use the vehicle in excess of this limit, we shall charge you extra as per rates mentioned above. In case of outside Dhaka, you get it for maximum of 10 hours’ time and 250 km run. The minimum charges will be applied only when you will use it for a period of maximum 3 hours or less time. Use of the transport for more than 3 hours will be considered as full day use. For booking of our transports, normally we need 50% advance payment.
